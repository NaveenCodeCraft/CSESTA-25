TECHNICAL EVENTS

Paper Presentation

A platform for showcasing innovative research ideas and technological advancements. Present your papers to a panel of distinguished experts and curious peers, receive valuable feedback, and inspire the next wave of innovation. Expand your horizons, share your findings, and make your mark in the world of science and technology.

Reverse Coding

In this event, participants are challenged with a given output and must work backward to reconstruct the original code logic. Sharpen your analytical skills, think critically, and decode the hidden algorithm behind the result!

Code Hunt

Dive deep into mysterious codes filled with hidden bugs and clever challenges. Be the first to crack them all and claim victory with your problem-solving prowess!

Quiz AI

Challenge your intellect in a thrilling quiz competition centered around Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Put your AI expertise to the test and prove your mastery in the evolving world of smart technologies!
